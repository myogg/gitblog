# [Steam Controller 外壳CAD开源文件说明](https://github.com/myogg/gitblog/issues/49)

This repository contains CAD files for the external shell (surface topology) of Steam Controller and the Steam Controller Puck, under a Creative Commons license.
 
This includes an STP model of each, an STL model of each, and an engineering drawing with critical features and keep-out areas for both devices.
 
Feel free to use these resources to design your own Puck holders, controller protective casings, or any other creative projects you would like to make!
 
Your Steam Controller belongs entirely to you, and you have the right to modify and use it as you wish.
 
That said, we highly recommend leaving internal modifications to professionals. Any self-inflicted damage will not be covered by warranty. More importantly, you risk breaking your Steam Controller, or even causing personal injury.
 
Please be careful, and enjoy your creation journey.
